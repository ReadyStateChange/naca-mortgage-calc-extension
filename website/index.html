Frequently Asked Questions

What is NACA?

NACA (Neighborhood Assistance Corporation of America) offers mortgages with no down payment, no closing costs, and no PMI for qualified buyers.

How accurate are the calculations?

Our calculator uses the same formulas as NACA's official calculator and includes real-time interest rates updated regularly.

What's the difference between the website and extension?

Both have identical functionality. The extension lets you use the calculator while browsing real estate sites without switching tabs.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. No personal financial information is stored or transmitted.
